What is CampusMap?
CampusMap is a student-led project developed by the Tinovation team at Cupertino High School. CampusMap offers students a new perspective of their schools, enables newcomers to understand how to go from place to place, and serves as a collective hub for school-related map info. CampusMap is a free tool that can be used with just an account.
Why do we help students?
"Fear of the unknown." As students, we understand how intimidated new students may feel upon being introduced to a new school; that is why we have developed a tool that will offer students more information about their school, classes, staff members, and overall environment in general.
Who are we?
Cupertino High School's "Tinovations" is a computer-based club. We are a group of hardworking students that are dedicated to helping others.
How can you help?
Upon creating an account, anyone will be able to upload scans. The process is simple—take pictures of your school and upload them under your account page. By doing this, future students will be able to learn more about their school.
